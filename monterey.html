Monterey


City Information

Monterey is a city located in the Central Coast region of California. Monterey is classified as a suburban city. Monterey has an estimated population of 29,571. The median household income is $85,365, which is more than the state median household income of $84,097. Monterey was incorporated on June, 14th 1890. (United States Census Bureau, 2022)

Fun Fact: Monterey was actually founded in 1770. It was the captial of Alta California for Spain and then Mexico. (Wikipedia, 2023).
