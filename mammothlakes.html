Mammoth Lakes


City Information

Mammoth Lakes is a city located in the Eastern Sierra Mountain range region of California. Mammoth Lakes is classified as a rural city. Mammoth Lakes has an estimated population of 7,093. Mammoth lakes median household income is $77,545, which is less than the state median household income of $84,097. Mammoth Lakes was incorporated on August, 20th 1984. (United States Census Bureau, 2022)

Fun Fact: Mammoth Lakes is located in mono county which is named after the mono people who are among the first to settle in the region. (Wikipedia, 2023)
