Sacramento


City Information

Sacramento is a city located in the Sacramento Valley region of California. Sacramento is classified as an urban city. Sacramento has an estimated population of 528,001. The median household income is $71,074, which is less than the state median household income of $84,097. Sacramento was incoporated on Feb. 27th, 1850. (United States Census Bureau, 2022).

Fun Fact: Sacramento is spanish for sacrament. (Wikipedia, 2023).
